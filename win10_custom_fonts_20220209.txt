1 - Download "WinFontsForALl" from "https://apps.gorilla.moe/winfontsforall/"
2 - unzip and copy dir to %USERPROFILE%\myprogs\
3 - create dir: %USERPROFILE%\Fonts\
4 - run %USERPROFILE%\myprogs\WinFontsForAll\setup.exe
5 - copy *.ttf into %USERPROFILE%\Fonts\
6 - make shure Windows Fonts For All Users" option to autostart is ticked!
7 - reboot, et Voila!
